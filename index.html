Doom & Bloom

Custom 3D​ game engine and networked multiplayer game made in 10 weeks. I contributed all ​3D​ graphics and a lot of gameplay code. Features Splatoon-like gameplay with collaborative texture painting over the network.

  • C++
  • OpenGL
  • GLSL shaders
  • Visual Studio
  • Blender

This was an uninstructed team project in the last quarter of school. I'm proud of this game because it is fun and achieves a certain look, given our time constraint and doing art assets myself. We did a good job of scoping our work for the time available so we were never caught without critical features or with an unstable or unperformant build. Watch the video to see it in action! Features include batched instanced draws and culling, LOD flowers, mocked subsurface scatter, interactive foliage and wind vertex shaders, networked virtual texture painting, first/third person controls.
