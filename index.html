2018

Custom C++ Game Engine multiplayer "Splatoon-like"

Doom & Bloom

C++, OpenGL

Custom 3D​ game engine and networked multiplayer game made in 10 weeks. This was still early in my graphics career so I was self-learning most of these concepts for the first time. I contributed the majority of ​3D​ graphics and a lot of gameplay code. Features Splatoon-like gameplay with collaborative texture painting over the network.

My contributions include:

  • networked virtual texture painting
  • batched instanced draws and culling
  • LOD
  • subsurface scatter shader
  • interactive foliage and wind vertex shaders
  • "soft" toon shader
  • first/third person controls.
  • particles

This was an uninstructed team project in the last quarter at UCSD. I'm proud of this game because it is fun and achieves a certain look, given our time constraint and limited knowledge at the time. Not to mention doing 3D models/texture assets myself. We did a good job of scoping our work for the time available so we were never caught without critical features or with an unstable or unperformant build. Watch the video to see it in action!
